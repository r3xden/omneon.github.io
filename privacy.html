Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Omneon is a fictional omniversal intelligence project presented through this website for informational, creative, and experimental purposes. Your privacy is respected as a core design principle. This Privacy Policy explains what data is (and is not) collected, how it is handled, and your rights as a user.

2. Data Collection Policy

Omneon does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data on external servers. There is no backend database, no cloud storage, and no hidden analytics system attached to this website.

3. Demo Login & Signup System (Local Storage Only)

If you choose to use the optional Login or Signup feature, the data you enter (such as email and username) is stored only within your own web browser using a technology called localStorage.

  • No passwords are transmitted to any server.
  • No data is shared with Omneon administrators.
  • No data is accessible to third parties.
  • Clearing your browser data will fully delete your demo account.

This system exists purely for interface demonstration purposes.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Omneon does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, third-party analytics, fingerprinting systems, or behavioral tracking of any kind.

5. External Links

This site may contain external links, including access to the Omneon AI on external platforms such as ChatGPT. Omneon is not responsible for the privacy practices, data handling, or content of external websites.

6. Children’s Privacy

Omneon does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no server-side data collection exists, there is no intentional data retention of any user demographic.

7. Data Security

Because Omneon does not operate backend servers or databases, risks related to data breaches, leaks, or unauthorized access are inherently minimized. However, users remain responsible for the security of their own devices and browsers.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect system changes or new features. All updates will be published on this page with a revised date.
